Visit Brum

\n

Your 1st destination to plan your 2nd city stay

\n\n\n\n\n
\n\n

Birmingham is the UK's second largest City. At the heart of the industrial revolution, Birminghams growth exploded in the 18th century turning a small market town into a bustling city of more than 1 million people. Managing to avoid the gaze of many\n tourists Birmingham is the perfect place to come experience the true multicultural reality that is the 21st century UK

Jewellery Quarter

\n
\n
\n
\n\n

The Jewellery Quarter is famous of course for its jewellery (in fact 1/2 of all jewellery sold in the UK is made here), but also for its redbrick warehouses and chilled out eateries and bars. Where better to enjoy a drink after\n a day of sight seeing than in the sun on one of the many rooftop terraces watching the world go by below.\n

Digbeth

\n
\n
\n

Having carried the tag of 'Up and coming' for many years Digbeth has recently been voted best place to live in the UK. Once the industrial heartbeat of Birmingham Digbeth is now better known for its thriving art scene. Here you\n can admire the grafitti art while traversing the many bars, micro-breweries and street food outlets now inhabiting the old warehouse spaces.\n
